Tour Info

I’m Giving A Talk!

I’ll be giving a presentation at the Brooklyn Historical Society on Friday October 21, 2016 at 6:30pm. The event starts at 5pm and includes a brewing workshop, a film screening, beer tastings, some amazing beer-related highlights from their collection, and more. My talk will be at 6:30pm. And it’s all FREE!

New Tour Date Scheduled!

The day after my talk, I’ll be giving another walking tour, on Saturday October 22, 2016 at 4pm. It will probably be the last tour of the season so get your tickets now!

Welcome to A Brewing History of Bushwick. Believe it or not, the neighborhood of Bushwick, Brooklyn was once the beer brewing center of America. On this walking tour, we’ll look at some historic 19th-century landmark brewery buildings that are still standing. We’ll also make our way to Brewer’s Row, recounting the stories of the breweries, the beer, and the people that made Bushwick a thriving brewing powerhouse.

And of course, we’ll end at a bar and enjoy a pint in the tradition of Bushwick’s once and future glory! The vibe is very informal and audience participation is encouraged. So come curious and come thirsty!

Tour Schedule

This tour is run through NYC H20, an awesome non-profit that educates New Yorkers about water, ecology, and the natural history of New York City.

The full tour schedule isn’t established yet but we hope to do it about once a month as long as weather permits.
